Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

In short: Double Shot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your videos stay on your device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Double Shot ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Double Shot is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs (IDFA)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Contacts, calendars, or other personal content
  • Your recorded videos or photos

3. Camera & Microphone Access

The App requires access to your device's camera and microphone solely for the purpose of recording video. This is the core functionality of the App. All video and audio data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Saved only to your local photo library
  • Never uploaded to any server

4. Photo Library Access

The App requests "Add Only" access to your photo library to save recorded videos. We do not read, access, or browse your existing photos or videos.

5. Subscriptions

Double Shot offers an optional VIP subscription managed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit framework. We do not process or store any payment information. All subscription transactions are handled by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles payment data.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. The only external communication is with Apple's App Store for subscription validation.

7. Data Storage

Subscription status is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard mechanisms (Keychain and iCloud Key-Value Storage) to sync your subscription across your devices. No data is sent to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we collect no personal data from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
